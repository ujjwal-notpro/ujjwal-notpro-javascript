Chocolate Cake Recipe

Preparation Time: 45 Minutes

Serving Size: 4 People

  • 2 Cups Flour
  • 1 Cup Sugar
  • 1 Cup Milk
  • 2 Eggs
  • Chocolate Powder
  • Butter

Step 1: Mix all dry ingredients.

Step 2: Add milk and eggs.

Step 3: Prepare smooth batter.

Step 4: Bake the cake for 30 minutes.
